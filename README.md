## 🚀 My Projects & Insights

**Titanic Survival Analysis**

🔍 **Objective**: Analyze passenger data and identify key factors influencing survival in the Titanic disaster.

🛠️ **Tools**: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI

📈 Process:

🧹 Cleaned and preprocessed passenger data by handling missing values and duplicates.
📊 Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify survival patterns.
🔎 Performed outlier detection and feature engineering for better insights.
📉 Created visualizations and dashboards to communicate findings effectively.

💡 Insights:

👩 Female passengers had significantly higher survival rates than males.
🏆 First-class passengers were more likely to survive compared to other classes.
👶 Children showed higher survival rates than adults and elderly passengers.
💰 Passengers paying higher fares had better chances of survival.

✅ Result: Generated actionable insights and interactive dashboards to understand survival trends and passenger demographics.

🤖 **Customer Churn Prediction**

🔍 **Objective:** Predict customer churn for a telecom service and identify factors driving customer attrition.

🛠️ **Tools**: Python, Power BI, Pandas, Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Scikit-learn

📈 Process:

🧹 Cleaned and preprocessed customer data by handling missing values and encoding categorical features.
📊 Conducted EDA to understand customer behavior and churn drivers.
🤖 Built and compared Logistic Regression and Random Forest models.
📈 Evaluated model performance using classification metrics and visualized results.

💡 Insights:

📱 Customers with specific internet service plans showed higher churn risk.
💳 Customers using auto-pay and long-term contracts had lower churn rates.
⚠️ Service-related issues were major contributors to customer attrition.

✅ Result: Enabled data-driven customer retention strategies through churn prediction and risk analysis.
